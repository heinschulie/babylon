  Risks & Trade-offs Accepted                                                                                                               
                                                                  
  - No retry on review failure — we accept that some trivially fixable issues will be skipped. Mitigation: data will tell us if this is a   
  problem, and adding a single retry is a one-line config change. 
  - Serializable-ready but not serialized — we're not building DB storage or dynamic composition now. We accept the risk of needing minor   
  refactoring later if that's needed.                                                                                                       
  - No compile-time enforcement of produces/consumes ordering — validated at construction time via Zod, not by the type system. Accepted
  because pipeline definitions are small and authored by us.
